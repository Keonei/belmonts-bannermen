Belmont's Bannermen

People at arms answering the call to game for good

Watch, laugh & engage! Belmonts Bannermen is a collection of video game players that will entertain you and for worthy causes.

The causes we fundraise for are chosen by the people who you see on screen. Heed the call as well as the Bannermen to help our friends in need. We promise to keep you entertained while we do it!

We want to provide a little community on a twitch stream, one marathon at a time. You can expect us to stream on Twitch.tv with some amount of regularity soon, and will keeping everyone updated via Twitter as to who we'll be supporting next. Hit that contact us button down at the bottom to make a suggestion!
